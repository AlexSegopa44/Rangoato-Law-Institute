Practice Areas

Explore Our Expertise

We provide comprehensive advisory services on all aspects of employment law in South Africa. From unfair dismissal disputes to disciplinary proceedings and CCMA referrals, we guide both employers and employees through every stage of the process with clarity and expertise. Our approach prioritises resolution, protecting your rights while minimising the disruption and costs associated with prolonged labour litigation.

  • Unfair dismissal and retrenchment disputes
  • Disciplinary hearings and representation
  • Employment contract drafting and review
  • CCMA and Labour Court referrals
  • Workplace policy compliance advisory
  • Unfair labour practice disputes

Our team offers expert guidance on civil wrongs — assisting clients in pursuing or defending claims arising from negligence, personal injury, and property damage. We conduct thorough damages assessments to ensure our clients receive just outcomes, while always exploring the fastest and most cost-effective path to resolution.

  • Negligence and liability claims
  • Personal injury and bodily harm matters
  • Damages assessment and quantification
  • Third-party and road accident claims
  • Medical negligence advisory
  • Property damage claims

We provide specialised advice on insurance strategy and dispute resolution, assisting both policyholders and insurers. From interpreting complex policy wording to disputing rejected claims, we help clients navigate the full spectrum of South African insurance law with precision, clarity and strategic intent.

  • Policy interpretation and coverage analysis
  • Disputed and rejected claim assistance
  • Bad faith insurer conduct advisory
  • Short-term and life insurance disputes
  • Ombudsman referrals and representation
  • Insurance risk management advisory

Matrimonial matters require a delicate balance of legal precision and human sensitivity. We guide clients through divorce proceedings, maintenance disputes, and asset division with discretion and care — always seeking the most amicable resolution possible to spare our clients the additional burden of hostile, drawn-out litigation.

  • Divorce proceedings and settlement agreements
  • Maintenance disputes and enforcement
  • Forfeiture of benefits advisory
  • Antenuptial contract review
  • Child custody and guardianship guidance
  • Division of joint estate

Sound contracts are the foundation of every successful business and personal arrangement. We draft, review, and advise on a wide range of commercial and personal agreements — providing strategic support when contractual disputes arise and practical solutions that protect your interests without unnecessary complexity.

  • Commercial agreement drafting and review
  • Breach of contract claims and defence
  • Contract interpretation disputes
  • Enforcement and debt recovery advisory
  • Service level agreement review
  • Legal opinions and risk assessment

Litigation is rarely the fastest or most cost-effective path to resolution. We pursue strategic negotiation, mediation, and arbitration to resolve disputes efficiently — protecting our clients from the financial and emotional toll of prolonged court battles. When court is necessary, we prepare and support you every step of the way.

  • Negotiation strategy and representation
  • Mediation facilitation and support
  • Arbitration proceedings advisory
  • Settlement agreement drafting
  • Pre-litigation dispute assessment
  • Court litigation when required
